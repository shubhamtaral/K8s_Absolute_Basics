kubectl get rs
Visibility into controller-level replica orchestration.
Example: kubectl get rs -n prod

kubectl describe rs <name>
Operational clarity into how a ReplicaSet is matching and managing pods.
Example: kubectl describe rs frontend-rs -n prod

kubectl scale rs <name> --replicas=<N>
Adjust capacity baselines.
Example: kubectl scale rs backend-rs --replicas=5

kubectl delete rs <name>
Terminate a ReplicaSet (and therefore its managed pods).
Example: kubectl delete rs myapp-rs

kubectl apply -f replicaset.yaml
Create or update ReplicaSets in a declarative, GitOps-friendly model.

kubectl get pods --selector=<label>=<value>
Audit which pods belong to a ReplicaSet.
Example:
kubectl get pods -l app=myapp
